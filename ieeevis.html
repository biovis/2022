BioVis at IEEE VIS

The half-day Biological & Medical Data Visualization Challenges Workshop (BioMedVis Challenges Workshop) will take place during the IEEE VIS 2022 conference (Oct 16, 2022).

Also this year we will conduct a BioMedVis challenge, incl. a Redesign Challenge: stay tuned for more details following very soon.

But mark your calendar already for the deadline of challenge submissions: September 1, 2022
